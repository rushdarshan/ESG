# EcoSphere

## Product Purpose
ESG management platform unifying Environmental measurement, Social engagement, and Governance assurance. Sustainability managers upload data, track carbon across Scope 1/2/3, engage employees via actions and leaderboards, and generate board-ready compliance reports.

## Users
- **Sustainability Manager** (primary): Uploads documents, monitors scores, generates reports. Needs clarity, data integrity, professional UI.
- **Employee** (secondary): Logs sustainable actions, earns XP/badges. Needs playful motivation, clear feedback.
- **CFO/Executive** (read-only): Reviews dashboard, compliance status. Needs at-a-glance summary, trust in data.

## Brand Tone
Professional and trustworthy without being dull. Modern and clean without being generic SaaS. Color conveys meaning (green = environment, blue = social, violet = governance). Every visual choice serves comprehension first.

## Anti-References
Avoid: gradient text, glassmorphism defaults, hero-metric templates, nested cards, side-stripe borders, stock-photo hero sections.
